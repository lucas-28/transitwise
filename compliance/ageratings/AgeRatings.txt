INFORMATION REGARDING USAGE OF THE SITE BY MINORS (from Privacy Policy)
Written by: Steven Macaluso

Our website is not directed at minors. We cannot distinguish the age of persons who access and use the site. If a minor has provided us with personal information with parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian should contact us. If we become aware of personal information being collected from a minor without he consent of a guardian, we will terminate the minor's account.

The circumstances in which we may need to collect information of minors includes: as part of a reservation and purchase of services.
