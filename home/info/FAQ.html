Frequently Asked Questions

What is this FAQ Page is about?

This page will help fill you in with your basic questions that may occur to you while purchasing a ticket to your next flight or any question that you may have.

Change your booking

If you want to change your booking or even cancel it, you can head towards your profile and check the status of your booking there and you can edit it, if you need to. However, you may have limited options for the time being as in to cancel your flight at the moment. If you need more support, you can email us at any time for more information about your flight status.

Get a refund

If you decided to cancel your flight, we will email you the status of your flight as well as refunding you back. Note that you will receive your refund winthin three business days after your cancellation.

Checking in before your flight

We will notify you 72 hours before your flight and send you a confirmation whether you are still attending your flight so that we can be sure to move you in quicker. If you fail to check in before 24 hours to your flight, you will to check in your flight at the airport.

Changing your account information

If you want to edit your profile after creating your account, you can do so by going to your profile where you signed in and edit any information that you need to change.
